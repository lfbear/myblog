After launching the new blog a few days ago, it felt pretty good, but it was missing some interactive elements. So I've updated it to version 1.1, adding a comments section! Registered users can comment directly, while comments from unregistered guests will require approval. This is to prevent our database from being flooded with spam~ 

<!--more-->

Now that I've started working, I might not have as many opportunities to write my own things. Most of the time, I'll probably only be able to jot down quick thoughts. Still, I'll make sure to use my free time to write cool stuff that either I love or you all enjoy~ I hope the friends of Little Flyer Bear will keep following me—don't forget! Registering an account and leaving comments on my blog is the absolute best way to stay in touch.
