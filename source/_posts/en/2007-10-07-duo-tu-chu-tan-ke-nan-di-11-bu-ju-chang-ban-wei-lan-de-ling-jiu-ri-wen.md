As a die-hard Conan fan, this was obviously an absolute must-watch. Overall, though, I felt it didn't quite live up to the previous movie—it was missing a lot of those nail-biting, heart-stopping moments. Plus, there were way fewer scenes exploring the relationship between Shinichi and Ran. M11 kicks off with a classic treasure hunt theme, only to wrap up with a "there is no treasure" twist. My strongest takeaway from M11 (Jolly Roger in the Deep Azure): it feels like a mashup of *Evan Almighty* (Pic 14) + *National Treasure* + *Titanic* (Pics 11-13). Quite a few scenes and shots felt heavily inspired by them. Let me post a few screenshots to show you what I mean.
