Without even realizing it, I've joined the ranks of the "Beijing Drifters" (北漂). Haha, thinking back, everything happened so incredibly fast. From sending out my resume, to the interview, the internship, and finally getting hired—the whole process was a whirlwind. 

This one-month internship really made me feel like I've grown up quite a bit. I had to figure out and handle almost everything by myself. Even though it wasn't my first time in Beijing, many of the things I observed were definitely worth reflecting on and pondering. I still remember the words written on the wall of my rented room: "Persevere" and "Strive". Seeing those words every single day always stirred up slightly different emotions in me. I think I'm going to love this new chapter of life, but before that kicks off, I should make sure to fully cherish and enjoy the rest of my college days~
