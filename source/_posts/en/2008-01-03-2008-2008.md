I can't believe 2008 is already here. It feels a bit sudden, yet full of things to look forward to. Looking back quietly at the path I've walked, I can really feel myself growing. Many of my perspectives have matured, but the motto at the core of my heart will never change: "Be true, be happy" (真自己，真快乐). I will continue to be myself and continue to experience joy! 2008, Do myself!
