What's in my heart?

In the midst of this chaotic industry competition, everyone fights for their own interests. Busy and exhausted~

Sometimes I daydream: if all of this suddenly vanished from the earth, leaving only humans, animals, and plants—reverting back to a primitive state—what would we actually have left?

Would the empty minds, the mechanical and repetitive actions, the suppressed voices, the repeatedly chanted slogans, the judgmental glares and whispers, and the hatred seeping from deep within our souls... would all of these disappear with it?

Those few precious things that remain in our hearts are things we absolutely must not lose.

This world isn't perfect. Humanity has the power to make her beautiful, but also to plunge parts of her into deeper darkness and cold. It all depends on the convictions that remain in the hearts of the majority of people in this world.
