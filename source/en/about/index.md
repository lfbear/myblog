## 🐻 Who is Little Flying Bear?

I fell down the internet rabbit hole back in **2001** and never looked back. In **2003**, I upgraded my online moniker from "Little Bear" to **"Little Flying Bear" (小飞熊)**, proudly claiming the title of the web's very first flying cub. 

Since graduating in **2008**, I've been hacking away at internet and software development. I've worn many hats and held various fancy titles over the years, but **"Software Engineer"** remains my absolute favorite.

> 💡 **Anti-counterfeit Stamp**: To avoid confusion with other flying bears roaming the web, I stamp my work and presence with the tag **lfbear**.
