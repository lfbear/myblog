Back to business: *Bobtail-Bear 2006 RC1* is still in my hands and hasn't been released yet. That's because... well, once you see RC1, you'll immediately spot the issues. Let me share a sneak peek regarding what everyone cares about the most: the open-source status of the code.

The frontend is open-source. As for the backend, we'll split it into an open-source version and a closed-source version. Starting from RC1, *Bobtail-Bear* users will need to register a free ID on the official website. Each ID corresponds to a free license certificate. A user's "reputation score" will determine whether they can download the open-source backend and receive free technical support. This reputation score is based on factors such as retaining the original copyright notice, submitting valuable feedback or suggestions for *Bobtail-Bear*, and other contributions to the project. After all, *Bobtail-Bear* shouldn't just be the work of Little Flyer Bear; it should belong to all of its users...

Alright, enough rambling! Keep your eyes peeled for the release of *Bobtail-Bear 2006 RC1*!
