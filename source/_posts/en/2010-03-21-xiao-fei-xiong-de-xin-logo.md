Without further ado, let's start with the image first:

[![Little Flyer Bear's New Logo](/assets/images/2010/03/logo-mid.gif)](/assets/images/2010/03/logo-mid.gif)

<!--more-->

This is the result of a certain someone's creative ideas, plus the two of us spending an entire day tweaking and revising it. Before this, I only had a surface-level understanding of Fireworks (FW). This time, it was a "learn-as-you-go" process—and though it took quite a while, it was incredibly rewarding! ~

I want to give a special shoutout to that "certain someone" (my girlfriend). After spending some time studying and practicing internet product design, she decided to use my website as a playground. Starting with the logo, she began by interviewing me to gather my requirements, analyzing the exact vibe I wanted to express, and figuring out how to closely tie it all in with a bear theme. And thus, Little Flyer Bear's new logo was born! Rather than just a logo, it feels more like a self-portrait of me, haha~
