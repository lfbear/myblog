The life motto I've believed in for the longest time is: "Be yourself, be truly happy." I always hope to find more joy in life, but reality forces you to consider and plan for the future. A new kind of pressure is quietly brewing in my heart. How should I manage the relationship between pressure and happiness? Carrying pressure in your heart can feel genuinely painful.

My recipe for happiness is: don't get lost, don't rush, slowly untangle the threads, and find a solution. Move toward your goals step by step. Every single step forward is progress in life, and every step is something to be celebrated.
